Dear Git,

I can still remember that one day
"git status" showed me you were single.
We both wanted a new beginning
so I hit "git add" and we started to mingle.
I was unsure about us at first
as hard as it is to admit
but I began to trust you with my changes
so I wasn't afraid to "git commit".
Your memory is spectacular
with git log you show me my things
and "git checkout" opens my commits
can you see the happiness you bring?
I don't fully understand your branches
but I have time to learn indeed
because I think we have quite a future
until something better comes across that I need.

From,
Eli
